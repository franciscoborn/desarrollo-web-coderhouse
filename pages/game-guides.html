Basics: How to Play 'Mini Mage'

Welcome to 'Mini Mage', where magic and adventure await! Here's a quick guide to help you get started on your journey:

Movement: Use the WASD keys to navigate your character through the mystical realms of 'Mini Mage'. Explore every corner of the map to uncover hidden treasures and secrets.

Combat: Engage in thrilling battles with enemies using a variety of skills and spells. Use the hotkeys (numbers 1 to 4) assigned to each skill to unleash devastating attacks on your foes.

Health Management: Keep an eye on your health bar during battles. When your health is running low, press the [Q] to use a healing potion and restore vitality.

Looting: Defeat monsters to collect gold and valuable loot. Simply approach the fallen enemies and press the [E] key to loot their remains and claim your rewards.

Treasure Chests: Keep an eye out for treasure chests scattered throughout the game world. Approach a chest and press [E] to open it and reveal the riches within. Be cautious, as some chests may be guarded by powerful creatures!

With these basics in mind, you're ready to embark on your epic adventure in 'Mini Mage'. May your spells be potent and your treasures plentiful!

Guide: Navigating the Menu in 'Mini Mage'

Welcome to the menu guide for 'Mini Mage', where you'll learn how to navigate the interface with ease. Follow these steps to explore the various options available:

1. Accessing the Menu: To open the menu, click on the 'Menu' button located in the top-right corner of the screen. This will reveal a dropdown menu with several options.

2. Exploring Categories: Within the menu, you'll find different categories such as 'Shop', 'Inventory', 'Skills' and more. Click on each category to expand it and reveal its subcategories.

3. Shopping for Items: Navigate to the 'Shop' category and click on it to reveal a list of available items for purchase. Use the scroll wheel on your mouse to browse through the selection. Click on an item to view its details and price.

4. Buying Potions: Within the 'Shop' category, locate the 'Potions' subcategory. Click on it to view a list of available potions, such as health potions, mana potions, and more. To purchase a potion, simply click on the 'Buy' button next to the desired item.

5. Purchasing Weapons: To buy weapons, navigate to the 'Weapons' subcategory within the 'Shop' menu. Browse through the selection of swords, staves, wands, and other weaponry. Click on the 'Buy' button to purchase the desired weapon.

6. Enhancing Items: Explore the 'Enhancements' subcategory within the 'Shop' menu to find items for enhancing your gear. From enchanting gems to strengthening runes, there's plenty to choose from. Click on the 'Buy' button to acquire the enhancement.

7. Confirming Purchases: After selecting an item to purchase, a confirmation window will appear. Review the details of your purchase, such as the item name and price, then click on the 'Confirm' button to complete the transaction.

With these steps, you'll be able to navigate the menu with ease and purchase potions, weapons, enhancements, and more in 'Mini Mage'. Happy shopping!
